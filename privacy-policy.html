Privacy Policy

App: Rabbiator · Developer: lijinihouse · Effective date: June 6, 2026

한국어 보기 ↓

1. Overview

lijinihouse ("we", "us") develops and operates Rabbiator (the "App") for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is used. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not require an account and we do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email, or contacts.

The App displays ads through Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK). To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • Device information (device model, operating system, language, coarse region)
  • App usage and diagnostic data related to ad delivery

Game progress such as your best score is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to us.

3. Advertising & Tracking

We use Google AdMob to show interstitial and rewarded ads. On iOS 14.5 and later, the App requests App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission before any tracking. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier at any time in your device settings.

Learn how Google uses data: Google Privacy Policy · How Google uses information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK (provided by Google LLC). Data handled by Google is governed by Google's own privacy policy linked above.

5. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will address it.

6. Data Retention & Security

We do not store your personal data on our own servers. Locally stored data (such as your best score) is removed when you uninstall the App.

7. Your Choices & Rights

Because we do not directly collect personal data, there is no personal account data for us to access or delete on our side. For advertising data, use the device-level ad controls described above and Google's controls. For any questions about your data, contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at lijinihouse@gmail.com.

1. 개요

lijinihouse(이하 "당사")는 iOS 및 Android용 게임 Rabbiator(이하 "본 앱")를 개발·운영합니다. 본 방침은 본 앱 이용 시 수집되는 정보와 그 이용 방법을 설명합니다. 본 앱을 이용함으로써 본 방침에 동의하는 것으로 간주됩니다.

2. 수집하는 정보

본 앱은 계정 가입이 필요 없으며, 이름·이메일·연락처 등 개인정보를 당사가 직접 수집하지 않습니다.

본 앱은 Google AdMob(Google Mobile Ads SDK)을 통해 광고를 게재합니다. 광고 게재 및 측정을 위해 AdMob이 다음을 수집할 수 있습니다:

  • 광고 식별자 (iOS의 IDFA, Android의 광고 ID)
  • 기기 정보 (기기 모델, 운영체제, 언어, 대략적 지역)
  • 광고 전달 관련 앱 사용 및 진단 데이터

최고 점수 등 게임 진행 정보는 기기 내부에만 로컬로 저장되며 당사로 전송되지 않습니다.

당사는 Google AdMob을 사용해 전면(인터스티셜) 및 보상형 광고를 표시합니다. iOS 14.5 이상에서는 추적 전에 앱 추적 투명성(ATT) 권한을 요청합니다. 거부하셔도 광고는 표시되지만 비개인화 광고로 제공됩니다. 광고 식별자는 기기 설정에서 언제든 재설정·제한할 수 있습니다.
